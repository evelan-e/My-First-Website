About Us

Our journey began in our cozy home kitchen. My mom has always been amazing at cooking and baking, but during the pandemic, she started experimenting with even more recipes. Everything she made was so delicious that our family and friends couldn’t stop adoring it. We all encouraged her to start selling her bakes—and that’s how her small baking business began.

As her passion grew, she started exploring new recipes, including bolu molen (a soft sponge cake filled with banana) and lapis legit (a rich Indonesian layered cake made with warm spices and incredible patience). Over time, she expanded her creations to include celebration cakes and fully customized designs with fondant or cream decorations—each one carefully made with love and attention to detail.

I created this website to help share her story and support her business. It’s a project that brings us closer as a family and allows more people to enjoy the joy and warmth she puts into every cake. We hope her bakes make your special moments even sweeter—just like they’ve done for us.
